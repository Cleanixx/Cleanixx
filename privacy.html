Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 3, 2026

1. Overview

Cleanixx is built with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data and do not sell or share your personal information with third parties.

2. Data We Collect

Cleanixx does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from your Mac. All system operations (storage optimization, disk analysis, app management, etc.) are performed locally on your device.

When you purchase a license through Paddle, Paddle collects your payment information and email address to process the transaction and deliver your license key. This data is governed by Paddle's Privacy Policy.

3. License Validation

When you activate Cleanixx with a license key, the app makes a secure request to the Paddle API to validate your license. No personal data is transmitted beyond the license key itself.

4. Analytics

Cleanixx does not use any analytics or tracking tools. We do not track your usage, behavior, or any activity within the App.

5. Cookies

The Cleanixx website (cleanixx.app) does not use tracking cookies. We use GitHub Pages for hosting, which may collect basic access logs.

6. Children's Privacy

Cleanixx is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at cleanixx@proton.me.
